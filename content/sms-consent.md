# SMS Message Consent

**Effective Date:** April 16, 2026

B&B Music World LLC operates KinderFile, a staff credential compliance tracking tool for daycare centers. By enrolling in KinderFile SMS reminders, you agree to receive automated text messages about upcoming staff credential expirations.

---

## Who Sends Messages

B&B Music World LLC (KinderFile), 9909 Locust Street, Glenn Dale, MD 20769

---

## Message Types

Automated reminders about CPR certification, background check, TB screening, and other credential expiration dates.

---

## Message Frequency

Message frequency may vary based on the number of credentials being tracked.

---

## Opting Out

To opt out: Reply **STOP** to any message to unsubscribe at any time.

## Help

For help: Reply **HELP** to any message.
